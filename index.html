Popular destinations in Egypt

Alexandria is one of the most famous places, and people come to it from all over the world. There are places such as the Montazah Palace gardens, Qaitbay Citadel, the beaches of Alexandria, Antoniadis Park, and the Roman Theater.

Sharm El-Sheikh has many natural landmarks: Naama Bay, Shark Bay, Ras Mohammed Reserve, Tiran Island, Sanafir Island, Nabq Reserve, and Gharqana. It also has tourist attractions: Al-Salam Mosque and the Sharm El-Sheikh International Museum

Tourist activities in the city of Dahab The city is characterized by many distinguished tourist and sports activities, as it is considered a destination for sports enthusiasts in general, especially skydiving and mountain climbing for surfing and sailboat enthusiasts. Natural and tourist attractions in the city of Dahab: Ras Abu Gallo area, the Blue Hole area, and the Asla area
